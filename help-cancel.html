How to cancel your PREZLO subscription

PREZLO subscriptions are managed through Apple. This guide shows you exactly how to cancel.

Before you cancel

When you cancel, you keep Pro access to PREZLO until the end of your current billing period. After that, your Pro subscription stops and you won't be charged again. You will continue to have free access to PREZLO with alerts on an approximately 15-minute delay.

Deleting the PREZLO app does not cancel your subscription. You must cancel through Apple.

Option 1 — Cancel through iPhone Settings

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap PREZLO
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
  6. Confirm when prompted

Option 2 — Cancel through Apple's website

You can also cancel directly from Apple's subscription management page:

Manage your Apple subscriptions →

Sign in with the Apple ID you used to subscribe to PREZLO, find PREZLO in the list, and tap Cancel Subscription.

Option 3 — Cancel through PREZLO

  1. Open PREZLO
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Plan & Billing
  4. Tap Manage Billing
  5. Tap Cancel subscription

This will take you directly to Apple's subscription management screen.

When to cancel to avoid being charged

Cancel at least 24 hours before your next renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period.

You can see your next renewal date in PREZLO under Settings → Plan & Billing → Manage Billing.

Free trial cancellation

If you're on the 7-day free trial, no action is needed to avoid being charged — the trial does not convert to a paid subscription automatically. Your trial end date is shown in PREZLO under Settings. If you would like to subscribe after your trial ends, you can do so from within the app.

After your free trial ends, you will automatically move to free access with alerts on an approximately 15-minute delay. No charge will be made unless you choose to subscribe.

Refunds

PREZLO subscriptions are purchased through Apple, so refund requests must go through Apple directly.

Request a refund from Apple →

Sign in with your Apple ID, find your PREZLO purchase, and submit a refund request. Apple will review and respond.

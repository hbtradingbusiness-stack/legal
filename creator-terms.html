• Use of ambiguous terms such as #partner, #collab, #ambassador, or #gifted without an accompanying clear statement of the commercial relationship

• Sole reliance on a platform-native paid-partnership or branded-content label without any in-Content language

7.4 Required Disclosure Language. You must include one of the following approved disclosures in every piece of eligible Content:

• Transactions attributed through fraudulent, manipulative, or unauthorized means, including click stuffing, cookie dropping, forced clicks, or manipulation of attribution identifiers

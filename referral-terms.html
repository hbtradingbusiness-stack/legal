Who Can Participate

Any PREZLO user with an active account in good standing may participate as a referrer, including users on a Free Trial and users on Freemium Access. You do not need to hold a paid Pro Subscription to send referrals or to earn rewards.

To qualify as a referred user, you must be a new user who has never previously installed the PREZLO Application and has not previously been associated with any PREZLO account on the same device.

The Trigger Event

A referral is considered successful when the referred user installs the PREZLO Application via your unique referral link and the installation is attributed to your referral link by PREZLO’s attribution system (currently Detour by Software Mansion). Installation of the Application via the referral link constitutes the trigger event. No payment by the referred user is required to generate a reward for the referring user.

The following do not independently trigger a reward:

• Installations not attributed to your referral link by PREZLO’s attribution system

• Installations on a device already associated with an existing PREZLO account

• Installations by users who have previously installed the Application on any device

PREZLO may withhold a reward pending completion of eligibility verification or fraud review.

The Reward

For every referred user whose installation is successfully attributed to your referral link, you earn one additional week (7 calendar days) of access. The reward is applied to your account as follows, based on your account status at the time the referral is attributed:

• If you hold an active Pro Subscription at the time of attribution: your Pro Subscription access will be extended by 7 days, managed internally by PREZLO without involvement of Apple or any third-party billing system.

• If you do not hold an active Pro Subscription at the time of attribution (including if you are on a Free Trial or Freemium Access): your free access period will be extended by 7 days.

Your reward tier is determined at the time the referred user’s installation is attributed to your referral link, not at the time you shared the link. Referral rewards are managed entirely by PREZLO and are not processed through Apple In-App Purchase, Apple Promotional Offers, or any third-party billing system. The referral reward is offered by PREZLO, not Apple. Apple is not a sponsor of, responsible for, or involved in granting referral rewards.

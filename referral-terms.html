PREZLO Referral Program Terms

These terms govern participation in the PREZLO referral program.

Last updated: May 2026

How it works: Invite a friend. If they subscribe through your link, you earn one free week of PREZLO access.

This program vs. the Creator & Affiliate Program

These terms govern the PREZLO consumer referral program only. The consumer referral program is open to all active PREZLO subscribers and rewards successful referrals with 7 days of free access delivered through Apple's Promotional Offer system.

PREZLO also operates a separate Creator & Affiliate Program for independent content creators. That program offers cash commissions (not service credits), is managed through GoMarketMe, and is governed exclusively by the Creator & Affiliate Agreement. The two programs are independent. Participation in the consumer referral program does not constitute participation in the Creator & Affiliate Program, and vice versa.

Who can participate

To send a referral, you must be a current PREZLO subscriber in good standing at the time the reward is evaluated.

To qualify as a referred user, you must:

  • Be completing their first paid subscription to PREZLO
  • Not have been previously associated with any PREZLO account on the same device or Apple ID

The reward

For every referred user who completes their first paid payment, the referring user earns 7 days of free PREZLO access delivered as a deferred renewal through Apple's Promotional Offer system. When redeemed, Apple defers the referring user's next billing date by 7 days.

The referred friend receives the standard free trial available to all new users. No additional benefit beyond the standard trial is provided to the referred friend.

Important: the reward does not change your Apple billing schedule beyond the 7-day deferral, and does not affect the refund process, cancellation process, or subscription-management rules. Apple continues to handle all billing, renewals, cancellations, and subscription management.

When the reward is granted

A referral is considered successful only when the referred user completes their first paid payment. For monthly subscribers with a free trial, this is the first renewal payment after the trial ends. For weekly subscribers without a free trial, this is the initial purchase payment.

The following do not trigger a reward:

  • Downloading the app
  • Creating an account
  • Starting a free trial without completing a paid payment

PREZLO may withhold a reward pending completion of eligibility verification.

Reward cap and expiration

  • Maximum of 56 days (8 weeks) lifetime per account
  • Accumulated reward days do not expire
  • Rewards may only be redeemed when you hold an active paid subscription and are not in a free trial period
  • Rewards have no cash value and cannot be redeemed without an active subscription

No cash value

Referral rewards are promotional service credits only. They have no cash value, are not transferable, may not be exchanged for money, and may not be combined with any other free trial or promotional offer. A referred user will receive the greater of the applicable offers, not both. If PREZLO's referral program is discontinued, accumulated but unredeemed reward days will not be compensated in cash or any alternative form.

How to share your referral link

When you share your referral link, you must clearly disclose that you may receive a benefit if the person you invite signs up. The default share message in PREZLO already includes this disclosure — do not remove or alter it.

Default share message: "Try PREZLO with my referral link. If you subscribe, I earn a free week: [link]."

If you share on social media, add a clear disclosure such as: "Referral link: I earn a free week if you subscribe to PREZLO through this link."

SMS and messaging

If you share a referral link by SMS, text message, email, or other direct message, you represent that you have obtained the recipient's consent before sending. You may not use automated systems, bots, or bulk messaging tools to distribute referral links.

Anti-fraud and clawback

PREZLO may withhold, revoke, or cancel referral rewards at any time if it detects or reasonably suspects:

  • Self-referrals
  • Fake or duplicate accounts
  • Device or Apple ID manipulation
  • Spam or misleading claims
  • Any other abuse of the referral program

PREZLO reserves the right to clawback rewards already granted if fraud is discovered after the reward was applied. Violation of these terms may result in removal from the program and termination of your account.

Apple continues to handle all In-App Purchase billing, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and subscription management. Nothing in the referral program modifies your relationship with Apple or Apple's terms.

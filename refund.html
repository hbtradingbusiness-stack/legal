Apple Handles Payments and Refunds

PREZLO subscriptions are purchased through Apple In-App Purchases. Apple is the merchant of record for those purchases, and Apple processes payments, renewals, cancellations, taxes, payment methods, and refund requests according to Apple’s own terms and policies. PREZLO does not directly charge your payment card, does not store your full payment-card information, and does not directly issue refunds for purchases made through Apple In-App Purchases. Refund eligibility and refund approval are determined by Apple.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a PREZLO subscription purchased through Apple In-App Purchases, you must submit the request to Apple. Go to https://reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with the Apple Account used for the purchase, choose "Request a refund," select the reason for the request, choose the PREZLO subscription or purchase, and submit the request. Apple, not PREZLO, determines whether a refund is approved. If Apple approves a refund, the timing of funds returning to your payment method is handled by Apple and your payment provider.

Unless you cancel your subscription through your Apple Account settings before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid subscription at the applicable price. Apple manages trial activation, renewal, cancellation, and billing through Apple In-App Purchases.

You are responsible for canceling before the next renewal date if you do not want your subscription to renew. Refund requests, if any, must be submitted to Apple through Apple’s refund process.

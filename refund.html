Digital Subscription Service; No Physical Returns

PREZLO is a digital subscription-based iOS app. PREZLO does not sell or ship physical products. There is no physical item to return, no packaging requirement, no return shipping address, and no exchange of physical goods. Access to PREZLO is provided digitally through the App and is managed through Apple In-App Purchases.

Apple Handles Payments and Refunds

PREZLO Pro Subscriptions are purchased through Apple In-App Purchases. Apple is the merchant of record for those purchases, and Apple processes payments, renewals, cancellations, taxes, payment methods, and refund requests according to Apple’s own terms and policies. PREZLO does not directly charge your payment card, does not store your full payment-card information, and does not directly issue refunds for purchases made through Apple In-App Purchases. Refund eligibility and refund approval are determined by Apple.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a PREZLO subscription purchased through Apple In-App Purchases, you must submit the request to Apple. Go to https://reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with the Apple Account used for the purchase, choose “Request a refund,” select the reason for the request, choose the PREZLO subscription or purchase, and submit the request. Apple, not PREZLO, determines whether a refund is approved. If Apple approves a refund, the timing of funds returning to your payment method is handled by Apple and your payment provider.

Free Trial

PREZLO offers a free trial period of 7 calendar days upon first installation of the Application. The Free Trial is managed entirely by PREZLO and is not processed through Apple In-App Purchase. No credit card, Apple ID payment authorization, or billing information is required to start or complete the Free Trial. The Free Trial begins on the date of your first installation and ends automatically after 7 calendar days based on your device identifier as recorded by PREZLO.

During the Free Trial period, no charge is made and therefore no refund is applicable. If you purchase a Pro Subscription during or after the Free Trial, that purchase is processed through Apple In-App Purchase and any refund request must be submitted to Apple. You are responsible for canceling your Pro Subscription through your Apple Account settings before renewal if you do not wish to be charged.

PREZLO does not guarantee any trading result, investment outcome, profit, loss avoidance, price movement, market reaction, execution opportunity, or alert performance. Any refund request for an Apple In-App Purchase must be submitted to Apple and is subject to Apple’s policies.

PREZLO’s Arena feature is a simulated trading competition that does not involve real money, real securities transactions, or real financial activity of any kind. Arena Positions are hypothetical only. No refund is applicable in connection with Arena participation, Arena results, Leaderboard outcomes, or League activity, as no charge is made for Arena use.

Cancellation Is Not the Same as a Refund

Canceling your PREZLO Pro Subscription stops future renewals but does not automatically refund amounts already paid. Subscription cancellation is managed through your Apple Account subscription settings. You are responsible for canceling before the next renewal date if you do not want your subscription to renew. Refund requests, if any, must be submitted to Apple through Apple’s refund process. Upon cancellation, your access will revert to Freemium Access.

PREZLO offers weekly and monthly Pro Subscription plans. Unless Apple determines otherwise under its policies, cancellation of a subscription stops future renewals and does not entitle you to a refund, credit, or prorated refund for any unused portion of a subscription period that has already been billed.

PREZLO manages your Free Trial and Freemium Access status internally using your Device ID and trial start date. Age Verification and Parental Consent Data

In jurisdictions where applicable law requires age verification or parental consent for app downloads or in-app purchases by minors — including Texas, Utah, Louisiana, and other applicable states — PREZLO relies on Apple's age assurance mechanisms and the Declared Age Range API to fulfill compliance obligations. PREZLO does not independently collect, store, or process government-issued identification, biometric data, or any other sensitive personal data for age verification purposes. Age category signals received from Apple through the Declared Age Range API are used solely to determine whether applicable age-related requirements apply to a user and are not shared with any third party, not used for advertising or profiling, and not retained beyond the session in which they are received. If a user is identified as a minor, PREZLO will apply any required restrictions in accordance with applicable law.

Community Features Data

If You use the community features of the Application, including commenting on or voting on market-activity alerts, PREZLO collects and processes the following information:

• Username: Your unique display name, either selected by You or automatically assigned. Usernames are stored in our database and are visible to other users within the Application.

• Comments: The text content of any comments You submit, the alert they are associated with, the timestamp of submission, and the number of times the comment has been reported. Comments are visible to other users within the Application.

• Votes: Your vote value (up or down) on a market-activity alert, the alert it is associated with, and the timestamp. Vote totals are visible to other users. Individual vote identity is not disclosed to other users.

• Block list: Usernames You have chosen to block are stored locally on Your device only and are not transmitted to PREZLO’s servers.

Community feature data is used to operate the community features, display content to users, detect abuse, enforce community standards, and respond to reports. PREZLO does not use community feature data to provide personalized investment advice or trading recommendations.
